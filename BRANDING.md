# Synapse Branding Guidelines

The Synapse name and its visual identity represent the official public identity of the Synapse Project.

These guidelines explain how the Synapse branding may be used to support community engagement while protecting the authenticity, consistency, and integrity of the project.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of these guidelines is to:

* Preserve the official identity of the Synapse Project.
* Prevent confusion regarding what is and is not an official Synapse project.
* Encourage community participation while protecting the project's visual identity.

These guidelines apply to the official Synapse branding regardless of where the assets are obtained from.

---

## Official Branding

The official Synapse branding includes, but is not limited to:

* The Synapse name.
* Official logos.
* Wordmarks.
* Icons.
* Wallpapers.
* Brand artwork.
* Official visual identity assets distributed by the Synapse Project.

---

## Permitted Uses

The Synapse Project encourages community members to reference Synapse in a truthful and respectful manner.

The official branding may be used for purposes such as:

* Reviews and product evaluations.
* Tutorials and educational content.
* Blog posts and articles.
* News reporting.
* Community discussions.
* Social media posts.
* Presentations.
* Screenshots of official Synapse software.
* Linking to official Synapse resources.

When doing so, the branding should be used only to identify or discuss the Synapse Project and should not suggest official endorsement or affiliation where none exists.

---

## Community Contributions

Community members are welcome to create and submit new visual assets through the official contribution process.

If accepted, contributed assets become part of the official Synapse visual asset collection and may be maintained, refined, or distributed by the Synapse Project.

---

## Prohibited Uses

Without prior written permission from the Synapse Project, you should not:

* Use the Synapse name or branding as the identity of another project, product, service, organization, or distribution.
* Modify official branding in a manner that changes or misrepresents the Synapse identity.
* Present unofficial material as official Synapse content.
* Use Synapse branding in a manner that implies endorsement, sponsorship, partnership, certification, or affiliation where none exists.
* Redistribute the official branding as an independent branding package or asset collection.
* Use the Synapse identity in a manner that may confuse users regarding the origin or authenticity of a product or service.

---

## Respecting the Identity

The Synapse branding exists to help users easily recognize official Synapse projects and communications.

Protecting the project's visual identity helps maintain consistency, trust, and authenticity across the ecosystem while still allowing the community to discuss, review, and share information about Synapse.

---

## Questions

If you are unsure whether a particular use of the Synapse branding is appropriate, please contact the Synapse Project before proceeding.

The Synapse Project appreciates community interest and aims to support legitimate educational, informational, and community use whenever possible.
