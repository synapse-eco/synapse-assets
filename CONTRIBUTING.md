# Contributing to Synapse Assets

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the visual identity of the Synapse Project.

The purpose of this repository is to develop, maintain, and preserve the official visual assets of the Synapse ecosystem. Every contribution helps improve the project's identity and consistency.

---

## Before You Contribute

Please ensure that your contribution:

* Is your own original work, or you have the legal right to contribute it.
* Does not infringe upon the copyright, trademark, or other intellectual property rights of any third party.
* Aligns with the design philosophy and visual identity of the Synapse Project.
* Is submitted in good faith for inclusion in the official Synapse asset collection.

---

## What Can Be Contributed

Contributions may include, but are not limited to:

* Logos
* Icons
* Wallpapers
* Theme artwork
* Illustrations
* Branding resources
* Promotional graphics
* Other visual assets related to the Synapse ecosystem

As the project evolves, additional categories may be introduced.

---

## Repository Structure

Please place new assets in the appropriate directory according to their purpose and format.

Whenever possible, include:

* Editable source files.
* Exported distribution formats.
* Appropriate filenames that clearly describe the asset.

Maintaining a consistent repository structure helps ensure long-term organization and maintainability.

---

## Review Process

All contributions are reviewed before becoming part of the official Synapse asset collection.

During review, a contribution may be:

* Accepted.
* Accepted with modifications.
* Returned for revision.
* Declined if it does not align with the project's goals, quality standards, or visual identity.

Acceptance is at the sole discretion of the Synapse Project.

---

## Contribution License

By submitting a contribution to this repository, you confirm that:

* You have the legal right to submit the contributed material.
* Your contribution is original or properly authorized.
* You grant the Synapse Project permission to review, modify, adapt, maintain, distribute, and include the contribution as part of the official Synapse asset collection if it is accepted.

Submitting a contribution does not guarantee that it will become an official Synapse asset.

---

## Code of Conduct

Please be respectful, constructive, and collaborative throughout the contribution process.

Design feedback should focus on improving quality, consistency, usability, and alignment with the Synapse visual identity.

---

## Thank You

Every accepted contribution helps strengthen the visual identity of the Synapse ecosystem.

Thank you for helping build Synapse.
